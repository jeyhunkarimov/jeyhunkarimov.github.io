Jeyhun Karimov

Researcher and Judoka from the Land of Fire.

I am a PhD student supervised by Tilmann Rabl and Volker Markl at Technische Universität Berlin, DIMA Group and German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI), IAM Group. I received my M.Sc degree at TOBB ETU, Department of Computer Science under supervision of Murat Ozbayoglu. In my thesis we collaborated with Provus/Mastercard to derive optimal ATM menu structure. Prior to that, I received my B.Sc degree at Middle East Technical University, Department of Computer Engineering. Also, I worked as a software engineer at Unify/Siemens EC, Mobicom, and Cyangate Inc.
My research interests include concurrent stream query processing and big data processing systems benchmarking.
