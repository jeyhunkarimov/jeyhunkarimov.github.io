Jeyhun Karimov

Engineer and Judoka from the Land of Fire.

I am working on query processing/optimization/scheduling topics with distributed data processing systems.
I obtained my PhD student supervised by Tilmann Rabl and Volker Markl at Technische Universität Berlin, DIMA Group. My PhD thesis focuses on data management, query optimization, and efficient stream data processing.
I received my M.Sc degree at TOBB ETU, Department of Computer Science under supervision of Murat Ozbayoglu. In my thesis we collaborated with Provus/Mastercard to derive optimal ATM menu structure. Prior to that, I received my B.Sc degree at Middle East Technical University, Department of Computer Engineering.

Currently, I am staff software engineer at Ververica. Prior to that, I worked at German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI), IAM Group, Huawei, Unify/Siemens EC, Mobicom, and Cyangate Inc.
